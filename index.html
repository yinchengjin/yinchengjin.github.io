About Me

Dr. Yincheng Jin is an assistant professor of Computer Science Department at Binghamton University. Before joining BU, I received Ph.D. Degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the State University of New York at Buffalo (UB), under the direction of Prof. Zhanpeng Jin at the Cyber-Med Laboratory.

My research spans the areas of Human-computer Interaction (HCI), Human-centered AI(HCAI), Accessibility, Huamn-AI interaction, and Healthcare, to make people be healthier and live in more intelligent environment. Towards this vision, I develop novel machine learning models and apply them to (1) enable human-centered AI, such as understanding human actions and people’s daily activities, and (2) advance health monitoring and develop new accessibility for deaf and hard-of-hearing (DHH) Community, etc.

I am looking for self-motivated students to join my research group towards Ph.D. and M.S study. If you are interested in my research, please send me an email with your resume/CV.
